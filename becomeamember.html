How Your Membership Gives Back

Joining our membership goes beyond personal benefits; it supports educational programs, internships, and community outreach at the museum. Your membership funds engaging exhibits, aids in conserving millions of global specimens, and contributes to our Community Access Program, which partners with local organizations to provide free museum passes to underserved groups.

Community Access Program

Each season members have the ability to join the Museum in supporting a local organization. UNSM will donate 2 free Morrill Hall passes for every membership purchased or renewed during the designated membership drive period. Additional passes can be purchased for donation at a discounted rate of 2 passes for $10.

Members are welcome to nominate a community organization to receive the passes. Other nominations are provided by UNSM staff, the Friends of the State Museum, or organizations can nominate themselves. Nominated organizations are evaluated based on their mission, audience served, and potential to provide access to community members who otherwise may not be able to visit museums.

Questions on the Community Access Program or organization nominations can be sent to unsmmembership@unl.edu.
