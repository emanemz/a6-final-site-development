Get Involved

There are plenty of ways to get involved with the museum.

Are you faculty or a researcher?

Use our Collections to support your research. Bring your classes to explore and learn. Share your research with the public by partnering with our education team on programs like Sunday with a Scientist.

Are you a member of the public or a fan of museums and natural history?

Volunteer at one of our public locations or in our Research Collections. Become a member and explore as often as you want. Consider supporting the museum financially so we can continue sharing science in our communities.

Regardless of how you got here, we're grateful for your interest and support.
